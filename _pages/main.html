Trustworthy Artificial Intellgence (TAI) Lab

We are a research lab in Machine Learning Group, School of Computing Science, Simon Fraser University leading by Dr. Linyi Li.

Our research aims to advance the general trustworthiness of artificial intelligence, with a focus on deep learning models empowering the state-of-the-art genAI such as language models. Our mission is to deliver AI technologies that are reliably aligned with human values, so as to minimize the negative impacts of powerful AI.

Specifically, we conduct research in:

  • Understanding AI in a principled way: We evaluate AI models with scientific benchmarks, advanced localization & attribution techniques, and theoretical frameworks to understand how model works and why existing trustworthiness threats exist.
  • Delivering certifiable mitigations: We are the leading team in developing certifiable mitigations for deep learning models so as the AI model comes with practical guarantees of trustworthiness when deployed in the wild world.

Our lab is located in the beautiful Burnaby campus of Simon Fraser University in the greater Vancouver area, Canada.
